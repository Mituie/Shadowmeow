Who is ShadowMeow?

ShadowMeow is a stealthy, feline-inspired vigilante with mystical powers. After being bitten by a stray cat, Lisa developed supernatural cat-like abilities, allowing her to move unseen in the night, strike with precision, and unleash a devastating sonic "Klaagmiauw." With a regal presence and unmatched agility, she rules the shadows.

Powers & Abilities

  • Shadow Prowl: She can merge into the shadows and move undetected.
  • Feline Resilience: She lands on her feet, heals quickly, and adapts to damage.
  • Klaagmiauw (Sonic Meow): A powerful, high-frequency screech that can disorient enemies.

Signature Look

ShadowMeow's iconic "Regal Cat Queen" outfit consists of a sleek black bodysuit with glowing golden lines resembling feline markings. She wears a flowing velvet cape with a midnight-blue interior that shimmers like a starry sky. Her mask enhances her piercing golden eyes, and her clawed rings serve as both decoration and weaponry.
